World's Best Cheeseburger Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • Chopped Lettuce
  • Sliced tomato
  • Sliced onion
  • Ketchup and mustard
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

1.) Preheat the grill or stovetop pan to medium-high heat

2.) Mixed slat and pepper wiith the ground beef and begin to knead the ground beef.

3.) Next Divide the ground beef into four equal portions and shape them into patties.

4.) Place the patties on the grill or in the pan and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.

5.) During the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheese to each patty and cover the patty to melt the cheese.

6.) While the patties are cooking, toast the buns on the grill or in a toaster for no more than 1 minute.

7.) Assemble the burger by placing lettuce, tomato and onion on the bottom bun. Add the cooked patty with melted cheese on top

8.) Add ketchup and mustard to the top bun, then place it on top of the patty.

9.) Enjoy your delicious cheeseburger!
